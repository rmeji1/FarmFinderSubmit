About Us:

FarmFinder

“A passion for the environment, community, and fine dining.” FarmFinder is a grass roots initiative that was started in order to support local farms, families, and eateries share the bounty of our land while building healthier communities. From appetizers to desserts, everything is better when it’s made with the freshest ingredients.

What farmers have been saying: “I’m proud to know that my vegetables are on the menu at the best local restaurants.” “None of our crops go to waste, and most of our produce gets prepared by chefs the very same day that we pick it.” “FarmFinder has put our small family farm on the map.”
